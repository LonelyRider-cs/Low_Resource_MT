39:4:001:1	The Coming Day of Judgment
39:4:001:0	The Lord of Heaven 's Armies says , “ The day of judgment is coming , burning like a furnace . On that day the arrogant and the wicked will be burned up like straw . They will be consumed — roots , branches , and all .
39:4:002:0	“ But for you who fear my name , the Sun of Righteousness will rise with healing in his wings . And you will go free , leaping with joy like calves let out to pasture .
39:4:003:0	On the day when I act , you will tread upon the wicked as if they were dust under your feet , ” says the Lord of Heaven 's Armies .
39:4:004:0	“ Remember to obey the Law of Moses , my servant — all the decrees and regulations that I gave him on Mount Sinai for all Israel .
39:4:005:0	“ Look , I am sending you the prophet Elijah before the great and dreadful day of the Lord arrives .
39:4:006:0	His preaching will turn the hearts of fathers to their children , and the hearts of children to their fathers . Otherwise I will come and strike the land with a curse . ”
