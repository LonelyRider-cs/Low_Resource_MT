17:10:001:1	The Greatness of Xerxes and Mordecai
17:10:001:0	King Xerxes imposed a tribute throughout his empire , even to the distant coastlands .
17:10:002:0	His great achievements and the full account of the greatness of Mordecai , whom the king had promoted , are recorded in The Book of the History of the Kings of Media and Persia .
17:10:003:0	Mordecai the Jew became the prime minister , with authority next to that of King Xerxes himself . He was very great among the Jews , who held him in high esteem , because he continued to work for the good of his people and to speak up for the welfare of all their descendants .
