66:15:001:1	Zheekat Gwich'in Khaiinkǫǫ Googwantrii T'ihee'yaa :
66:15:001:0	Ąįįtł'ęę zheezhit gwiindal gwahchy'aa gwintsii geegwaragoolii kwaa gwał'in . Zheekat gwich'in seven nąįį googwantrii seven goontą' , ąįį khaiinkǫǫ kwaii , k'iighai' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa khaiinkǫǫ zhyaants'įį t'ihee'yaa geh'an .
66:15:002:0	Ąįįtł'ęę chųų choo nahdrin inju' gwaraah'in ts'ą' kǫ' haa nihteerinjaa k'it t'inchy'aa nał'in . Ąįįts'ą' chan zhit juu Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa k'injigiighit haa nin iizųų ch'atthąįįn choh ts'ą' vik'itiltsį' ts'ą' voozhri' ch'eechyaa haa danakhwatł'oo kwaii gaiiyiigwiłtsąįį nąįį chan nał'in . Chųų choo nahdrin inju' gwaraah'in eeghaii , nagadhat , ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa harps goovantł'inlii gootą' .
66:15:003:0	ts'ą' Moses vich'ilik Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vitsyaa , ts'ą' Divii Gii ch'ilik kwaii igiilii : “ K'eegwaadhat , Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa jiintsii , nigwitr'it łyaa nitsii ts'ą' nizįį ! Nahkat gwats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat inlii , nigwitr'it łi'didlii ts'ą' gwik'it gwanlii !
66:15:004:0	K'eegwaadhat juu nąįį datthak łyaa nahaanjat ts'ą' deenagahah'aa ! Juu dee nijiintsii zhyaa k'it t'ahoonjyaa ? Nan zhrįh shroodiinyąą inlii . Nahkat datthak nats'ą' giheedaats'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa deenagahah'aa , deeni'in datthak łi'deegwidlii gwagwah'in geh'an . ”
66:15:005:0	Jii tł'ęę zheekat Kharigidiinjii Zheh nihky'aa t'igwiizhit , ąįį gwizhit zheh gwadhah shroodiinyąą nał'in .
66:15:006:0	Zheekat gwich'in seven nąįį googwantrii seven goontą' ąįį Kharigidiinjii Zheh gwats'an chigiinjil , ch'adak ik linen daagąįį nizįį ch'ąhndit nagaazhii ts'ą' goodrihtsii chan gwach'aa chyah gold nilii dakhwachaa .
66:15:007:0	Ąįįtł'ęę ąįį gwandaii dǫǫ ( 4 ) Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa iłtsąįį ch'ihłee zheekat gwich'in seven nąįį tyah ghoo gold seven zhit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa zhyaants'įį deedąą'ąį' goovantł'eiin'ąįį . Adan t'ee khit ts'ą' khit geenjit gwandaii .
66:15:008:0	Kharigidiinjii Zheh , łat needaangwat ąįįt'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vich'eegwąhndit ts'ą' vat'aii haa ts'an t'igwii'in , ts'ą' ch'ihłee gaa duuyeh Kharigidiinjii Zheh nihdąhkhaii . Dahthee googwantrii seven ąįį zheekat gwich'in seven nąįį zhak nigiinlii gęhdaa tr'igwiheedhaa gwats'ą' .
