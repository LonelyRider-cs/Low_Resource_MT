42:24:001:1	Jesus Neegwiindaii
42:24:001:0	Drin zhit vanh dąį' hee tr'injaa nąįį ąįį jidii gwinzii vagwaatsan shriginlik haa Jesus vatth'ank'it gwats'ą' gahaajil .
42:24:002:0	Vatth'ank'it gwadąįį kii gihłeehiltth'at łee googwąąh'in .
42:24:003:0	Ąįįts'ą' nihdeeginjil , gaa Diik'eegwaadhat Jesus vichį' gąąh'in kwaa .
42:24:004:0	Zhat nigiilzhii ts'ą' deegwiizhik t'igwii'in gaagiindaii kwaa , gwiizhit khan hee dinjii neekwaii vagwach'aa ch'ahdrii giveeghaii nadhat nagwaanąįį .
42:24:005:0	Gwint'aii gąąnjat ts'ą' nankat nikiigogwąh'ee gwiizhit ąįį zheekat gwich'in neekwaii jyaa goovahnyąą , “ Dinjii gwandaii gwiizhit jaghaii tth'ank'it gwizhit vinkeegǫh'in ?
42:24:006:0	Dzaa dhidii kwaa t'inchy'aa , gwandaii needhidlit . Galilee dhidii dąį' , deenakhwahnyąą ganoondaii .
42:24:007:0	‘Gwidinji' dinjii iizųų nąįį antł'eehahchyaa ts'ą' gigiihahtsak . Ąįįtł'ęę drin tik gwiindhat tł'ęę neegweheendaii . ’ ”
42:24:008:0	Ąįįtł'ęę tr'injaa nąįį giiginjik anaanjik ,
42:24:009:0	Tth'ank'it gwats'an oo'ee neegiijil ts'ą' jii datthak giitsyaa 11 chan ts'ą' ch'izhii nąįį haa googwaandak .
42:24:010:0	Ąįį tr'injaa t'ee Mary Magdalene , Joanna , James vahan Mary , nąįį haa , Jesus vitsyaa nąįį ts'ą' goovandah deegwiizhik goovaagogwaandak .
42:24:011:0	Gaa vitsyaa nąįį ąįį tr'injaa deeginyąą zhyaa t'igwinyąą giindhan ts'ą' gwik'iinjigiighit kwaa .
42:24:002:1	Emmaus gwats'ą' Geedaa
42:24:012:0	Gaa Peter ąįį ndak ninehjin ts'ą' tth'ank'it gwats'ą' hilgik , gwizhit gwąąh'in , gaa yagwach'aa zhrįh nah'in , gaa ch'akwaa . Ąįįtł'ęę dizheh gwits'eehoozhii , deegwiizhik t'igwinyąą li' niindhan .
42:24:013:0	Izhit drin gootee dinjii neekwaii nąįį Jerusalem gwats'an 7 mile Emmaus kwaiik'it gwats'ą' gee'al .
42:24:014:0	Deegwiizhik datthak geenjit nihłaa geegiginkhii .
42:24:015:0	Geegoogwaandak ts'ą' geegiginkhii gwiizhit Jesus gooveeghaih niinzhii ts'ą' goovaa ahaa ,
42:24:016:0	giiyąąh'in gaa adan t'inchy'aa giiyaandaii kwaa .
42:24:017:0	Jesus t'agoovahnyąą , “ Oh'al gwiizhit jidii eegoohkhii ? ” Khaihłan nagadhat ts'ą' goonin kat tr'igwidii dha'aii .
42:24:018:0	Ch'ihłak dinjii Cleopas oaazhii ąįį yuahkat , “ Nan zhrįh Jerusalem gwizhit aakin inlii juk k'ehdąį' gwanaa gwats'an deegwiizhik gaanandaii kwaa ? ”
42:24:019:0	“ Jaghaii , deegwiizhik ? ” goovahnyąą . “ Jesus , Nazareth gwats'an deeginlik reh , ” ginyąą . “ Ąįį dinjii t'ee łyaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nilii , dee'ya' chan ts'ą' deiinyą' datthak geenjit t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa chan ts'ą' dinjii datthak haa giishoo'ąhłii .
42:24:020:0	Giinkhih kįh dilk'ii nąįį chan ts'ą' Israel kįh dilk'ii nąįį tr'ahaahkhwaa eenjit gwintł'eegiiyahchįį tł'ęę t'ee gigiiyiłtsak .
42:24:021:0	Ąįįts'ą' Israel gwich'in nąįį neehahshii ji' , tr'oonyaa eenjit veenjit niinjich'aradhat ! Izhit gwizhrįh nakwaa , juk t'ee k'ehdąį' gehnaa hee t'igwiizhik .
42:24:022:0	Diitee gwats'an tr'injaa lat łyaa geegwaroolii kwaa diineenjit t'igiizhik . Vanh gineech'ihii'aii gwiizhit , tth'ank'it gwats'ą' gahaajil .
42:24:023:0	Gaa khyų' giichį' kantii . Oo'ee neegiijil ts'ą' zheekat gwich'in diits'ą' igwiilkįį ts'ą' tth'aii hee Jesus gwandaii diigahnyąą .
42:24:024:0	Diilak nąįį zhat nagaajil akhai' ąįį tr'injaa deegiinyą' chy'aa tth'aii nihk'it t'oonchy'aa googwąąh'ya' , gaa adan ąįį giiyąąh'ya' kwaa . ”
42:24:025:0	Tł'ęę Jesus jyaa goovahnyąą , “ Łyaa tr'agǫhjik , deegwahtł'oo zhyaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii deegiinyą' gaa gwik'iinjuhkhit kwaa !
42:24:026:0	Christ Diik'eegwaadhat jyaa ts'ą' t'ee khaiinjich'ohoodhat , ąįįtł'ęę geh'an t'ee zheekat gwizhit vich'eegwąhndit . ”
42:24:027:0	Ąįįts'ą' Jesus Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit deevaraiinyą' datthak eegoovaagwaandak , oondaa nijin Moses vidęhtły'aa chan ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nąįį dagogwąąntł'oo izhit gwats'an hee .
42:24:028:0	Nijin kwaiik'it gwats'ą' geedaa geenigiinjil dąį' Jesus ąįį gehgoo heekhaa k'it t'ii'in .
42:24:029:0	Gaa zhak giiyuunjik ts'ą' jyaa giiyahnyąą , “ Diinaa niinkhaa . It'ee niighit kwaa tǫǫ gweheelyaa . ” Ąįįts'ą' t'ee goovaanihdineezhii ts'ą' naahaa .
42:24:030:0	Vakat ch'ara'aa ts'ą' goovaanaadii , łųhchy'aa oonjik ts'ą' “ Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa mahsį' , ” nyąą . Ąįįtł'ęę łeeyiiłnaii ts'ą' goovantł'eeyahtsit .
42:24:031:0	Jesus t'inchy'aa łee giiky'aanjik , gaa goovandah hiljii naanaii .
42:24:032:0	Jyaa nihłagaanyąą , “ Nijin dzaa gwats'ą' tr'eedaa dąį' , Dęhtły'aa Choh kat geediinaagwaandak gwiizhit łyaa shoo diizhit dha'aii k'it t'oonchy'aa . ”
42:24:033:0	It'ee zhat nineegehjin ts'ą' Jerusalem gwits'eegahoo'oo , izhit t'ee gah vitsyaa 11 nąįį chan ts'ą' ch'izhii nąįį łigiiljil ts'ą'
42:24:034:0	jyaa ginyąą , “ Diik'eegwaadhat neegwiindaii t'oonchy'aa . Simon Peter ts'ą' vigwiilkįį ! ”

42:24:035:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee ąįį dinjii neekwaii nąįį gee'al gwiizhit goots'ą' deegwiizhik chan ts'ą' Diik'eegwaadhat łųhchy'aa nihtr'aahnaii jyąhts'ą' juu nilii giiky'aanjik geegoovaagogwaandak .
42:24:036:0	Ąįį dinjii neekwaii nąįį jii kwaii geegoovaagwaandak gwiizhit khanhee Diik'eegwaadhat gootee nadhat naanaii ts'ą' jyaa goovahnyąą , “ Tsinehdan nakhwaa oolį' ! ”
42:24:037:0	Łyaa gihilghaa ts'ą' gąąnjat , ch'anky'aa t'inchy'aa giiyuunyąą .

42:24:039:0	Shakwai' , shanli' haa nǫh'in ąįįtł'ęę shįį t'ihchy'aa shaakhwahandaii . Tth'an , shatthąį' haa shakat noonjii ji' t'ee gwahǫǫh'yaa , duuyeh ch'anky'aa jyaa t'inchy'aa . ”
42:24:040:0	Jyaa nyąą ts'ą' danli' , dakwai' haa goots'ą' agwaahch'in .
42:24:041:0	Googaa tth'aii gwik'iinjigiighit kwaa , gaa łi'didlii giiyaandaii haa goozhit shoh dha'aii , ąįįts'ą' Jesus goovahkat , “ Dzaa nik'ee tr'ihee'aa khwa'įį ? ”

42:24:043:0	ts'ą' goovandah iin'al .

42:24:045:0	Dęhtły'aa Choh kat gwigweheetth'ak eenjit t'aih goots'an iłtsąįį ,
42:24:046:0	tł'ęę jyaa goovahnyąą , “ Jii t'ee gwadanakhwatł'oo : Christ khaiinjich'iheedhat , ąįįtł'ęę drin tik gweheedhaa ts'ą' neegweheendaii .
42:24:047:0	Dęhtły'aa Choh kat vitsyaa nąįį giiginjik eech'oohąąhchyaa gwinyąą . Nakhwatr'agwaanduu akhagoiinyąą tł'ęę Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa neenjit oo'an gweheelyaa . Jerusalem gwizhit tr'ookit jii gwandak gwintł'eegihee'aa ąįįtł'ęę dohju' hee jii nankat dinjii datthak chan .
