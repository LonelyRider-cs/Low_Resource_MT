42:18:001:1	Tr'injaa Vakai' Niindhat ts'ą' Dinjii Ch'aroahkat Zheh gwizhit Ch'oahkat haa Googwandak
42:18:001:0	Ąįįtł'ęę Jesus jii gwandak zhit geegoovąąhtan , jidii geenjit khadigigiinjii akhagagooheenjyaa kwaa eenjit .
42:18:002:0	“ Zhit gwa'an kwaiik'it goo'aii gwizhit dinjii ch'aroahkat zheh gwizhit ch'oahkat nilii . Ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa aanjat kwaa ts'ą' dinjii chan hił'ee kwaa nilii .
42:18:003:0	Ąįįts'ą' chan tr'injaa vakai' niindhat chan zhat kwaiik'it gwich'įį . Ąįį gwintł'oo yeeninidik ts'ą' deeyiindhan gwik'it t'igwehee'yaa geenjit yaandeegwii'ąįį . ‘Juu shats'ą' iizųų nąįį eenjit shits'iinyąą . ’
42:18:004:0	Łyaa niighyuk yik'eegwahthat kwaa , gaa gohch'it jyaa adaanyąą , ‘Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa chan aaljat kwaa ts'ą' dinjii chan hooł'e' kwaa gaa ,
42:18:005:0	Jii tr'injaa vakai' niindhat tr'ihkhit shik'eegoodhat niindhan geenjit shaandeegwii'ąįį akwat vik'eegwahałthat . Ąįį akwaa ji' , gwintł'oo geenjit shaandeegwehee'aa ts'ą' chishahąhndaa ! ’ ”
42:18:006:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee Diik'eegwaadhat t'agoovahnyąą , “ Jii dinjii ch'aroahkat zheh gwizhit ch'oahkat tsinteech'ąąhkaii dohtth'ak .
42:18:007:0	Ąįį eenjit , Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ąįį dinjii tǫǫ drin haa , ‘Shits'iinyąą , ’ yahnyąą nąįį nihteetr'anaazhik lee t'inchy'aa ? Jidii eenjit , ‘Shits'iinyąą , ’ giiyahnyąą , ąįį eenjit khan goots'iheenjyaa lee t'inchy'aa ?
42:18:002:1	Pharisee ts'ą' Dinjii Tax Eenjit Laraa Oonjii haa Gwaandak
42:18:008:0	Nakhwaagwahaldak , zhit deegiindhan gook'eegwahahthat t'inchy'aa gaa nijin Gwidinji' nankat k'idik ji' gwik'iinjigwiighit agwahah'aa shrǫǫ ? ”
42:18:009:0	Jesus jii gwandak ąįį juu dinjii ihsįį indhan ts'ą' juu datthak gooveenjit iizųų nąįį haa gwaandak .
42:18:010:0	“ Dinjii neekwaii nąįį Kharigidiinjii Zhee Gwachoo gwats'ą' gahaa'oo , khagigeheejyaa geenjit . Ch'ihłee Pharisee nilii ts'ą' ch'ihłee chan tax eenjit laraa oonjii nilii .
42:18:011:0	Jii Pharisee adąhzhrįh nadhat ts'ą' jyąhts'ą' khagidiinjii , ‘Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa mahsį' , juu nąįį datthak k'it shahzhrįh idininjich'aldhat kwaa , niinjigwalzhii kwaa , ts'ą' tr'injaa veedhiidii kwaa ąįį haa dhiichįį kwaa , mahsį' ! Jii tax eenjit laraa oonjii vik'it t'ihchy'aa kwaa .
42:18:012:0	Drin ch'ats'ateets'įį neekwaii gwizhit drin neekwaii ch'ih'aa kwaa , ts'ą' jidii oihjii datthak ts'an ch'ihłoaatin kwaii ( 1/10 ) Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vats'an ałtsii . ’
42:18:013:0	Gaa zhit tax eenjit laraa oonjii ąįį geh'at gwa'an adąhzhrįh nadhat , gaa zheezhit k'iidak gaa gwąąh'in kwaa , gwiizhit didrihtsii gwahgwat ts'ą' t'inyąą , ‘Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa , neeshraahchy'aa shǫįįnyąą , tr'agwaanduu tr'agwał'ya' ! ’
42:18:003:1	Jesus Chan ts'ą' Tr'iinin Tsal Nąįį haa
42:18:014:0	Jii tax eenjit laraa oonjii t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haa vaa gwinzii goodlit ts'ą' dizheh gwits'eehoozhii dąį' . Gaa Pharisee ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haa vaa gwinzii kwaa . Ąįį geh'an juu ch'andaa adoodiinyąą , t'ee heetsal , ts'ą' juu ch'andaa adoodiinyąą kwaa t'ee heetsii , ” Jesus nyąą .
42:18:015:0	Dinjii lat nąįį digitr'iinin tsal Jesus gook'aa nahaandal eenjit giits'ą' haadlii . Vitsyaa nąįį googwąąh'in dąį' geenjit ch'ats'ą' t'agoogahnyąą .
42:18:016:0	Gaa Jesus ąįį tr'iinin nąįį aanaii ahnyąą ts'ą' jyaa nyąą , “ Tr'iinin shats'ą' hijyaa nąįį , zhyaa goovoh'in , K'eegwaadhat Veegwinii'ee t'ee dinjii tr'iinin k'it veegoo'aii nąįį gaa ts'an t'agwarahnyąą .
42:18:004:1	Dinjii Valaraa Gwanlii
42:18:017:0	Jii ganoondaii ! Juu K'eegwaadhat Veegwinii'ee oonjii kwaa t'ee tr'iinin tsal k'it veegoo'aii ts'ą' duuyeh zhat gwats'an ilii . ”
42:18:018:0	Israel kįh nilii ąįį Jesus oahkat , “ Geech'ǫąąhtan nizįį , khit gwarandaii shi'yaa ji' geenjit deeshi'yaa goo'aii ? ”
42:18:019:0	“ Jaghaii dinjii nizįį shaiinyąą ? ” Jesus yuahkat . Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa zhrįh nizįį t'oonchy'aa .
42:18:020:0	Moses va-law kat jyaa deegwinyąą gaakhwandaii . ‘Nakąį' akwat na'at nilii kwaa haa oonchį' shrǫ' ! Dinjii ęhdaa tr'agoon'aa shrǫ' ! Ch'ooan'įį shrǫ' ! Oonts'it ts'ą' dinjii ts'ą' gadǫǫhtrit shrǫ' ! Nahan , niti' haa gook'eegwąhthat ! ’ ”
42:18:021:0	Ąįį dinjii jyaa nyąą , “ Tr'iinin ihłii dąį' gwats'an Moses va-Law kwaii k'eegwałthat . ”
42:18:022:0	Nijin jyaa nyąą Jesus yiitth'ak dąį' t'iiyahnyąą , “ Jii kwaii tth'aii t'ini'yaa goo'aii , nizheh gwits'eehindii ts'ą' nik'eiich'ii datthak laraa t'įį hinlii ts'ą' ąįį laraa neeshraahchy'aa nąįį teiinlii . Jyaa dinizhik ji' zheekat gaayiigwahantsyaa neenjit gwehee'aa . Ąįįtł'ęę shits'eehindii ts'ą' shitsyaa inlii . ”
42:18:023:0	Gaa nijin ąįį dinjii jii gwiitth'ak dąį' , łyaa tr'igwidii yiłtsąįį , valaraa gwanlii geh'an .
42:18:024:0	Jesus tr'igwidii dhidlit yąąh'in ts'ą' jyaa yahnyąą , “ Dinjii valaraa gwanlii K'eegwaadhat Veegwinii'ee ts'an heelyaa eenjit gogwantrii t'oonchy'aa !
42:18:025:0	Nin nitsii Camel ąįį tahtsal keeheehaa ąįį gogwantrii kwaa gaa dinjii valaraa gwanlii K'eegwaadhat Veegwinii'ee nihdeeheehaa eenjit veegogwantrii . ”
42:18:026:0	Juu zhat dilk'ii yiitth'ak nąįį giiyųąhkat , “ Juu neeheezhii gǫ' ? ”

42:18:032:0	Israel gwich'in nilii kwaa nąįį intł'eehaachyaa , ąįį nąįį t'ee giiyeeheedlaa ts'ą' giich'itsyaahaa'ee ts'ą' giik'aa heezhree .
