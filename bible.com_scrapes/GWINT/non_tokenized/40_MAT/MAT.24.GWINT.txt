40:24:001:1	Jesus, Jerusalem Kharigidiinjii Zheh Gwachoh An Gweheelyaa Geeginkhii
40:24:001:0	Nijin Jesus, Kharigidiinjii Zheh gihłeehoozhii ts'ą' gwa'an tr'ineezhii dąį' vitsyaa nąįį giits'ą' haajil ts'ą' jii Kharigidiinjii zheh gwachoh ts'ą' geeghaih gwa'an zheh goodlii haa gwąąh'in giiyahnyąą. 
40:24:002:1	Gwigweheetrii ts'ą' Khaiinjich'ireheeghit
40:24:002:0	Gaa Jesus goodii ginkhe', “Aahą', jii kwaii datthak gwinzii nǫh'in. Jii łi'haa nakhwaagwaldak, Yeendaa ji' kii tsal ch'ihłak gaa neekwat dehee'aa kwaa ts'ą' gwideek'it hee'aa kwaa. Datthak an heelyaa.” 
40:24:003:0	Jesus, Taih Vakat Olives Nahshii kat dhidii gwiizhit vitsyaa nąįį gweentak giits'ą' haajil ts'ą' giiyųąhkat, “Zhat nigwiindhat ji', deegwehee'yaa ts'ą' deegweheenjyaa diinaa gwandak. Nijin neiindyaa ts'ą' ndaagwahąąn'ąį' gwats'ą' nahgwan ji' deegwehee'yaa gwarahąąh'yaa?” 
40:24:004:0	Jesus t'agoovahnyąą, “Gwits'į' adak'ǫǫntii! ts'ą' neenakhwaroon'ee shrǫ'! 
40:24:005:0	Dinjii lęįį nąįį nakhwats'ą' giheedaa ts'ą' ‘Shįį t'ee Christ (Messiah) t'ihchy'aa!’ giheenjyaa ts'ą' veenjit gihkhii t'inyąą giheenjyaa ts'ą' dinjii lęįį nąįį neegeheen'ee t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:006:0	Nihłiriighan gwahohtth'ak ts'ą' yeenjik niighit gwa'an nihłiriighan gwinyąą gwahohtth'ak, gaa geenjit nakhwadrii zhit neets'ąįįneegoojyaa shrǫ'! Jyaa dagweheenjyaa goo'aii, gaa ąįį nan ndaanąą'ąį' gweheelyaa t'igwii'in kwaa. 
40:24:007:0	Nahkat gwanlii ts'ą' geegwinii'ee gwinlęįį chan nihłigiheeghaa. Nijuk datthak chan nan haatrat, ts'ą' dinjii chan ihdeeheengaii. 
40:24:008:0	Ąįį kwaii datthak t'ee tr'injaa tr'ookit tr'iinin haa tr'iłts'ik k'it t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:009:0	“Ąįįtł'ęę nakhwagooheendal ts'ą' khaiinjich'anakhwagahahkhit geenjit gwantł'ee nakhwagahahchyaa ts'ą' nakhwęhdaa tr'igwigwehee'aa. Ts'ą' shęh'an nahkat datthak nakhwatr'igehee'ee. 
40:24:010:0	Lęįį nąįį zhat nigwiindhat ji' digwik'injigwiighit akhagagooheenjyaa, gwantł'ee nihłagahaachyaa ts'ą' chan łyaa nihłitr'igihee'ee. 
40:24:011:0	Ąįįtł'ęę Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii adaa'įį lęįį nąįį gweheelyaa ts'ą' dinjii lęįį nąįį neegeheen'ee t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:012:0	Izhit ji', dinjii lęįį nąįį tr'agwaanduu googwitr'it gweheelyaa, ts'ą' lęįį nąįį nihłeenjit ch'eet'igwiniindhan hee'yaa kwaa. 
40:24:013:0	Gaa juu ndaagwąą'ąį' gwats'ą' datthak gwik'injigwiighit di'įį t'ee neeheezhii t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:003:1	Gęhdaa Kwaa Tr'agwaanduu T'igwehee'yaa
40:24:014:0	ts'ą' jii Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Veegwinii'ee, Gwandak Nizįį haa nankat datthak geech'agoohahtan, dinjii nąįį datthak shik'injigiheeghit, ąįįtł'ęę nan ndaagwahąąn'ąį' nagwahaadhal.” 
40:24:015:0	“Ąįį Kharigidiinjii Zheh gwizhit shroodiinyąą goo'aii ąįį, ‘Jidii tr'agwaanduu gwiizųų,’ Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vakharigidiinjii zheh shroodiinyąą kwaa gwahtsii zhat gwizhit nin'ąįį. Ąįįt'ee Daniel Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nilii geenjit gwadąąntł'oo t'inchy'aa.” (Ąįįts'ą' juu jii dęhtły'aa nah'in nąįį t'ee deegwinyąą t'igwinyąą gwinzii gaakhwandaii gwat'oohchy'aa, t'oonchy'aa.) 
40:24:016:0	“Jyaa digwiizhik gwagwahkhan zhit juu Judea nahkat gwizhit gwich'įį nąįį taih kat gwats'ą' gal gahahdaa gwizhrįh goo'aii. 
40:24:017:0	Zhit juu dizheh gwakat doodii gwiizhik jyaa digwiizhik ji' dik'eiich'ii neehoondal geenjit dizheh nihdanǫǫzhi' shrǫ'. 
40:24:018:0	Zhit juu gwanzhįh deek'it gwitr'it t'agwah'in gwiizhik jyaa digwiizhik ji' ch'adak ik nooheendal geenjit dizheh gwits'eehoozhi' shrǫ'. 
40:24:019:0	Izhit ji' łyaa tr'injaa vizhit tr'iinin chan ts'ą' juu tr'iinin ach'ahma' nąįį haa eenjit gwigweheetrii t'oonchy'aa! 
40:24:020:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vats'a' khagadǫhjii Neegwaazhii Drin akwat khaii gwanlii gwiizhit t'igweheenjyaa kwaa geenjit! 
40:24:021:0	Zhat nigwiindhat ji' googwantrii nagwahaadhal, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa jii nan iłtsąįį dąį' gwats'an ch'ihłok gaa jyaa dagwahtł'oo googwantrii gwaraah'in kwaa, akwat chan ch'ihłok gaa jyąhts'ą' neegwarahaan'yaa kwaa t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:022:0	Gaa K'eegwaadhat, drin gwitee gwagwaanchy'aa nahgǫǫ neegwiłtsąįį kwaa ji' jii nankat dinjii nąįį datthak an heelyaa t'oonchy'aa. Ąįį dinjii gwitee tr'iinlii nąįį jyaa dagwahkhyuk khaiinjich'igiheeghit kwaa eh'an drin gwiteegwagwaanchy'aa nahgǫǫ niłtsąįį t'inchy'aa. 
40:24:023:0	“Ąįįts'ą' chan, ‘Dzaa Christ dhidii nǫh'in!’ akwat ‘Oo'at dhidii t'inchy'aa!’ ąįį dinjii ch'ihłee jii jyaa nahnyąą ji' gaa gwik'injuhkhit shrǫ'! 
40:24:024:0	Christ adagaa'įį ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa eenjit ginkhii ihłii nyąą nąįį gwigwee'in ts'ą' geegwaroolii kwaa t'igihee'yaa haa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa dinjii gwitee tr'iinlii nąįį neegeheen'ee gaa gach'agwadhan ji' gwizhrįh t'igihee'yaa t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:025:0	Gwik'it t'igweheenjyaa gwehkįį nakhwaagwaldak t'ihnyąą, geenjit shoodǫǫhk'įį! 
40:24:026:0	“Akwat dinjii nąįį, ‘Zhit gooh'in nangwinjir gwa'an dhidii t'inchy'aa,’ ginyąą ji' gwats'ą' hohjyaa shrǫ'. Akwat chan ‘Dzaa gooh'in, geegoh'ik ch'an'įį łee!’ ginyąą ji' gaa gook'injuhkhit shrǫ'. 
40:24:027:0	Nijin Gwidinji' dzaa gwits'eehidii ji' East gwats'an West gwats'ą' nahtan kǫ' haadhak k'it zheetii datthak ch'eegwąhndit nagwahaadhal t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:004:1	Gwidinji' Nineeheedyaa
40:24:028:0	“Jii k'it t'igweheenjyaa t'oonchy'aa, nijin ch'atthąį' iłchįį t'ee zhat gwa'an vultures (deetrya' k'it t'inchy'aa) nąįį łaahaadaa t'oonchy'aa.” 
40:24:029:0	“Googwantrii gwiizųų gehgoo goodhat gwagwahkhan shree ch'ahahdrii kwaa nahaadhal ts'ą' tǫǫ oozhrii chan ch'ahahdrii kwaa, ts'ą' są' chan zheekat gwats'an niheedaa. Ts'ą' zheetii gwa'an jidii dhidlii kwaii datthak haatrat. 
40:24:030:0	Gwidinji' nats'ahts'ą' vagwahaakyaa Jesus jyaa nyąą ąįį gwik'it zheetii vagwahaakyaa, ąįįtł'ęę nankat gwich'in nąįį datthak Gwidinji' zheekat, gwats'an zheek'ǫh zhit vat'aih ts'ą' vich'eegwąhndit jiintsii haa k'iidąą needyaa ji', tr'igwidii haa treeyah hee'aa gwiizhit giiyahaah'yaa. 
40:24:005:1	Ts'iivii Fig Nahshii Ąįį Haa Geech'ǫąąhtan
40:24:031:0	Trumpet, vach'arahłii nilii gwint'aii haahchik nagwahaadhal, gwiizhit dizheekat gwich'in nąįį dzaa nankat gwizhit datthak gwats'ą' nagavaheelyaa ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa juu gwitee tr'iinlii nąįį datthak khaihłan nigiheelyaa t'oonchy'aa.” 
40:24:032:0	“Zhit ts'iivii Fig vakat nahshii, ąįį vats'an gwagoo'ee. Nijin vatthoochan tth'aii hee datłok ts'ą' vakat ch'at'an tsal khan nahshii dąį', it'ee shin gwats'ą' nahgwan gaakhwandaii. 
40:24:033:0	Izhit gwik'it deegwehee'yaa diinyą' gwik'it t'igwii'in gooh'in ji' t'ee yee'at tthał nii'ee nihdeegweedii gwats'ą' ahaa vaakhwandaii. 
40:24:034:0	Łi'haa nakhwaagwaldak t'ihnyąą! Juk gweendaa gwandaii nąįį datthak niheedhaa kwaa ts'ą' jii kwaii zhit gweheedhaa. 
40:24:006:1	Nijin Drin ąįįts'ą' Nijin Ji' T'igweheenjyaa Ąįį Ch'ihłak Gaa Gaandaii Kwaa
40:24:035:0	Zhee, nan haa kwaa gweheelyaa gaa shiginjik ąįį khit łi'didlii heelyaa.” 
40:24:036:0	“Ch'ihłak gaa nijin drin akwat nijin ji' t'igweheenjyaa gaandaii kwaa, zheekat gwich'in nąįį ts'ą' Gwidinji' haa gaagiindaii kwaa gaa Gwiti' zhrįh gaandaii t'oonchy'aa. 
40:24:037:0	Gwidinji' k'inidik ji' deenaadąį' Noah vaadeegwiizhit chy'ah ąįį gwik'it t'ineegweheenjyaa. 
40:24:038:0	Zhat drin gwanaa dąį', nankat datthak chųų teehaankhaii nagwahaadhal gwats'ą' dąį' dinjii nąįį ch'aga'aa ts'ą' ch'igiinįį, dinjii ts'ą' nich'it haa nihłagoonjii googwahtsii, dahthee Noah tr'ihchoo zhit heekhaa gwats'ą' datthak jyaa digee'ya'. 
40:24:039:0	Googaa aiizhit dąį' deegwii'in gaagiindaii kwaa, gaa deegwii'in gwik'igaanjik dąį' gookat chųų t'iizhit ts'ą' chųų datthak goovoonjik. Nijin Gwidinji' k'inidik ji', jyaa digweheenjyaa. 
40:24:040:0	Jyaa digwii'in gwiizhit dinjii neekwaii nąįį gwanzhįh deek'it tr'agwagwah'in, Gwidinji' ch'ihłak ąįį neehoondal ts'ą' ch'ihłak ąįį zhat akhahoonjyaa. 
40:24:041:0	Tr'injaa neekwaii nąįį, łųhchy'aa vaatr'ahtsii haa tr'agogwah'in, gwiizhit Gwidinji' ch'ihłak neeyahoondal ts'ą' ch'ihłak ąįį zhat akhahoonjyaa. 
40:24:042:0	Nijin drin K'eegwaadhat neeheedyaa gwak'ǫǫhtii kwaa ji' gaakhwandaii kwaa t'oonchy'aa! 
40:24:043:0	Gaa jii gaakhwandaii! Ąįį dinjii nijin tǫǫ gwa'an juu ch'an'įį yizheh łeegwahahnaii gaandaii ji' yeenjit nagoohaah'yaa ts'ą' duuyeh yizheh łeegwahnaii t'inchy'aa. 
40:24:007:1	Juu Dinjii Eenjit Gwitr'it T'agwah'in Iizųų ts'ą' Nizįį
40:24:044:0	Jyąhts'ą' geh'an nakhwan chan oohadhanzhii gwizhrįh goo'aii, Gwidinji' geehoohłii kwaa gwiizhit k'ineeheedik t'inchy'aa.” 
40:24:045:0	“Juu yats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat eenjit yitsyaa nilii t'ee vigwitr'it gwinzii ts'ą' chan goonzhįį? Ąįį t'ee juu yats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat ch'izhii ditsyaa nąįį k'eehahtyaa yahahtsyaa ts'ą' drin gwiteegwaanchy'aa zhat nineegwiidhak dąį' gooch'ahah'aa geenjit. 
40:24:046:0	Nijin juu yats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat oo'ee neezhįį ts'ą' jyaa dii'in yah'in ji' łyaa gwintł'oo yashoohahłyaa t'inchy'aa. 
40:24:047:0	Łyaa, nakhwaagwaaldak! Ąįį juu yats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat jidii veegoo'aii datthak k'eehahtyaa yahahtsyaa. 
40:24:048:0	Gaa ch'itsyaa iizųų nilii ji' adats'ą' giheehkhyaa ts'ą' jyaa diheenjyaa, ‘Shats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat niighyuk oo'ee neeheedyaa kwaa t'oonchy'aa.’ heenjyaa. 
40:24:049:0	ts'ą' juu yaatr'agwah'in nąįį dahkhwaii ts'ą' ch'anakhwanį' nąįį haa ch'a'aa ts'ą' ch'iinįį. 
40:24:050:0	Nijin ji' juu yats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat k'ineeheedik gaandaii kwaa geh'an geeneeyuulii kwaa gwiizhit yarahtee k'ineeheedik. 
40:24:051:0	Jidii tsal nilii ts'ą' khandiyeheent'ii ts'ą' juu gwik'injiighit adaa'įį nąįį haa niyahahchyaa, izhit t'ee gwintł'oo heetree ts'ą' dagho' nihts'ą' łǫǫn'al haa neeyik'ii k'it t'ihee'yaa.”
