40:17:001:1	Jesus Ch'ijuk Vigweheechy'aa ts'ą' Vaa Ch'eegwąhndit
40:17:001:0	Drin nihk'iitik gwahaadhat ąįįtł'ęę Jesus, Peter, John ts'ą' John vachaa James nąįį haa ddhah choh kat tthan goo'aii gwa'an gwats'ą' goovahaadlii. 
40:17:002:0	Aakin giiyaa'įį gwiizhit Jesus ch'ijuk t'inchy'aa naanaii, gwiizhit vinin shree k'it ch'ąhndit dhidlit, ąįįts'ą' vagwach'aa it'ee shrit daagąįį ts'ąįį vakat gaa ch'ąhndok haadhak.  
40:17:003:0	Ąįįtł'ęę vitsyaa tik nąįį deenaadąį' gwats'an Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii neekwaii, Elijah ts'ą' Moses haa Jesus ts'ą' giginkhii googwah'in. 
40:17:004:0	Ąįįts'ą' Peter, Jesus t'ahnyąą, “K'eegwaadhat, dzaa tr'iilk'įį geenjit łyaa gwinzii gwik'it t'iindhan ji' dzaa nakhweenjit tr'ał zheh tik gwarahahtsyaa, ch'ihłak ąįį neenjit, ts'ą' ch'ihłak chan Moses veenjit, ts'ą' ch'ihłak chan Elijah veenjit.” 
40:17:005:0	Peter, tth'aii hee ginkhii gwiizhit zheek'ǫh ch'ąhndit gook'aa t'iizhit ts'ą' zheek'ǫh zhit gwats'an gwideezhuh jyaa nyąą gwigwiitth'ak, “Jii t'ee Shidinji' t'inchy'aa, veet'iihthan ts'ą' łyaa shooshahtsii t'inchy'aa oodǫǫhk'įį!” 
40:17:006:0	Nijin vitsyaa nąįį zhit gwideezhuh giitth'ak dąį', łahchy'aa gwint'aii gihiłghaa ts'ą' nankat diginin gwagwaa'ee ts'ą' jyąhts'ą' oo'an gahaatł'it. 
40:17:007:0	Jesus goots'ą' haazhii ts'ą' gookat naanjik ts'ą' t'agavahnyąą, “Ndak nineekhwakhin ts'ą' noojat kwaa.” 
40:17:008:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee k'iidak gwagwaah'in akhai' Jesus zhrįh nadhat łee gwagwah'in. 
40:17:009:0	Ddhah kat gwats'an k'iizhak neegeedaa gwiizhit Jesus t'agoovahnyąą. “Nakhwandee ts'ą' gwigweelkįį gǫǫh'ya' łyaa ch'ihłak gaa haa goondak shrǫ', dahthee Gwidinji' ch'ichį' ts'an neegweheendaii gwats'ą'!” goovahnyąą. 
40:17:010:0	Ąįįtł'ęę vitsyaa nąįį giiyųąhkat, “Jaghaii Law eech'ǫąąhtan nąįį Elijah tr'ookit k'ineeheedik ginyąą t'iginyąą?” 
40:17:011:0	Ts'ą' t'agoovahnyąą, “Elijah adan tr'ookit k'ineeheedik ts'ą', adan jidii datthak shrigweheelyaa ts'ą' tr'iilęįį nigwehee'aa geenjit. 
40:17:012:0	Gaa, nakhwaagwahaldak, Elijah ch'adąį' hee k'inidik t'inchy'aa, googaa dzaa nankat gwich'in nąįį juu t'inchy'aa giiyaandaii kwaa ts'ą' dooł'ya' giiyuunyąą gwik'it datthak t'igiiyah'ya'. Ąįį gwik'it chan Gwidinji' khaiinjich'agahahthat t'igiinchy'aa.” Jesus goovahnyąą. 
40:17:002:1	Jesus, Chyaatsal, Ch'anky'aa Iizųų Vizhit Shrininlik
40:17:013:0	Ąįįtł'ęę vitsyaa nąįį ąįį John chųų gwats'an ąhtsii eeginkhii t'inyąą łee giiky'aanjik. 
40:17:014:0	Nijin zhit dinjii nąįį łeeljil, k'ineegiidal dąį' dinjii ch'ihłak Jesus ts'ą' niinzhii ts'ą' yats'ą' nikiigwiintthaii, 
40:17:015:0	ts'ą' t'iiyahnyąą, “K'eegwaadhat, shigii chyaatsal sheenjit neeshraahchy'aa oiinyąą! Epileptic naandoo ts'ą' oo'an t'eehaadhat nilii ts'ą' nijin veenjit gwiizųų nineegwaadhat dąį', chųų akwat kǫ' haa zhit nineet'aadhat. 
40:17:016:0	Ąįį geenjit nitsyaa nąįį ts'ą' hoołchįį gaa khyų' shrineegiiyilii.” yahnyąą. 
40:17:017:0	Jesus ditsyaa nąįį jyaa dahnyąą, “Łyaa gwik'iinjuhkhit kwaa ts'ą' gwiizųų t'oochy'aa łee it'ee nakhwaashandaii goodlit! Deegwąhkhyuk zhyaa nakhwaa t'ihihchy'aa shoohnyąą? Ąįį chyaatsal dzaa shats'ą' hohchįį!” Gavahnyąą. 
40:17:018:0	Jesus zhit ch'anky'aa iizųų, gwint'aii ts'ą' khagiinkhee, gwagwahkhan zhit chyaatsal ts'an khaiinzhii, ts'ą' zhat gwagwahkhan chan chyaatsal shranaazhik. 
40:17:019:0	Ąįįtł'ęę vitsyaa nąįį dinjii nąįį kwaa gwiizhit giiyųąhkat, “Jaghaii khyų' ąįį ch'anky'aa iizųų kharahchįį t'oonchy'aa?” giiyahnyąą. 
40:17:020:0	Jesus t'agoovahnyąą, “Nakhwak'injigwiighit natsal geh'an khyų' t'akhwa'in. Jii t'ee łyaa nakhwaagwaldak t'ihnyąą, nakhwak'injigwiighit jii mustard seed natsal ąhtsii ji' jii ddhah dzaa gwats'ą' hinkhaii vaiinyąą ji' gaa gwik'it t'ihee'yaa t'inchy'aa, ąįįts'ą' nakhwak'injigwiighit zhit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vat'aii haa deesho'ya' nohthan datthak gwik'it t'akhwa'yaa t'oonchy'aa.” 
40:17:003:1	Jesus Chan Hee Niheedhaa Geeginkhii
40:17:021:0	Jii ch'anky'aa iizųų k'it t'inchy'aa t'ee khadigiriinjii ąįįts'ą' khadigireheejyaa geenjit adach'ara'aa kwaa haa gwizhrįh khahohchyaa t'oonchy'aa,” yąą. 
40:17:022:0	Nijin vitsyaa nąįį Galilee nahkat khaihłan nigiinjil dąį' Jesus jyaa gavahnyąą. “Gwidinji' tr'ahaahkhwaa geenjit dinjii nąįį antł'eeyahahchyaa ts'ą' 
40:17:004:1	Kharigidiinjii Zheh, Laraa Gwintł'eereheelyaa Goo'aii
40:17:023:0	giiyęhdaa tr'igwihee'aa t'inchy'aa, gaa drin tik ąįįtł'ęę neegweheendaii.” Vitsyaa nąįį łahchy'aa gwiintł'oo tr'igwigwidii nagaanaii. 
40:17:024:0	Nijin Jesus ąįįts'ą' ditsyaa nąįį haa Capernaum kwaiik'it k'eegiidal dąį', dinjii Kharigidiinjii zheh geenjit tax laraa oonjii nąįį ąįį Peter ts'ą' gahaajil ts'ą' giiyųąhkat, “Nakhwageech'ǫąąhtan Kharigidiinjii zheh gwats'ą' lee tax ookwat t'inchy'aa?” ginyąą. 
40:17:025:0	“Jyaa dihee'yaa gwizhrįh t'inchy'aa!” Peter gavahnyąą.Nijin Peter zheh nihdineezhii dąį' Jesus t'iiyahnyąą, “Simon jii geenjit deeyiindhan, juu dzaa nankat kings nąįį ts'ą' tax ookwat t'oonchy'aa? Juu zhat gwich'įį nąįį akwat juu zhat gwich'įį kwaa nąįį?” yahnyąą. 
40:17:026:0	“Juu dzaa gwich'įį kwaa nąįį reh”, Peter yahnyąą.“Akwaa ji' juu dzaa gwich'įį nąįį gooheekwat kwaa goo'aii łee. 
40:17:027:0	Gaa, dzaa gwich'in nąįį goonehts'į' t'igwehee'yaa gwits'į', oo'an van ts'ą' hiinkhaii, ts'ą' cheehjal dha'aii ts'ą' ąįį jidii łuk tr'ookit khahchįį ąįį vighit gwąąh'in, gwizhit Kharigidiinjii zheh geenjit tax laraa t'aahchy'aa agwahaa'aa t'oonchy'aa, ąįį goovantł'eiinlii, nan, ąįįts'ą' shįį haa diineenjit.” Jesus yahnyąą.
