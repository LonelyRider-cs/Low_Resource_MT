48:4:001:1	Paul, Galatians Nąįį Eenjit Tr'igwidii
48:4:007:0	Izhit gwats'an it'ee duuyeh slave ǫhłįį ts'ą' vigii ąįį hohłyaa. Ąįįts'ą' vigii dhoodlit gwats'an Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa jidii digii nąįį eenjit di'įį ąįį datthak nakhwantł'eeheelyaa. 
48:4:008:0	Gwehkįį yeenii Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haakhwandaii kwaa, ąįį geh'an ch'anky'aa iizųų, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nilii kwaa nąįį slaves ǫhłįį. 
48:4:009:0	Gaa juk Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haakhwandaii dhoodlit akwat jii ąįį jyaa danakhwahaihjyaa, juk Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa naandaii goodlit. Ąįį ch'anky'aa iizųų goot'aii kwaa ts'ą' ginilih kwaa haa ginlii, jii nankat gwats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat ąįį jaghaii goots'eekhwajyaa nohthan? 
48:4:010:0	Drin akwat, shin, khaii, khaiits'ą', shreenyaa, neegwadadhat kwaii gwiteegogwąąnchy'aa khagadǫhjii goo'įį. 
48:4:011:0	Nakhweenjit naljat jidii nigwitr'it t'agwał'ya' datthak zhyaa geenjit gwakwaa nakhweenjit lee t'ishizhik? 
48:4:012:0	Shachaa nąįį, nandaa, haa łyaa Jews nąįį Law-zhit nakhwats'ą' k'eegaroodhat shrǫ'! Łyaa gwik'it t'ahohchy'aa kwaa eenjit nokhwałkat t'ihnyąą, shįį ąįį shats'ą' k'eedeegwaraadhat kwaa. Yeenii chan shats'ą' gwiizųų nakhwagwitr'it gwiinli' kwaa. 
48:4:013:0	Jaghaii tr'ookit dąį' gwats'an Gwandak Nizįį eenoaałtan gaakhwanandaii, dhałts'ik geh'an. 
48:4:014:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vizheekat-gwich'in nakhwazheh nihdǫhchįį ts'ą' vak'ǫǫhtii gwik'it gwintł'oo nakhwaadhałts'ik gaa nihk'it nakhwazheh shoo haa nihdeeshohchįį, sh'atr'oo'ee kwaa ts'ą' cheeshohǫǫhchįį kwaa, Christ Jesus nihdǫhchįį gwik'it t'ashoolik! 
48:4:015:0	Łyaa nakhwaashroonchy'aa chy'aa nakhwanaldaii! Deegwiizhik gǫ'? Nakhwandee shantł'eehoh'aa ji' gaa gwik'it t'akhwa'yaa reh nakhwaashandaii! 
48:4:016:0	Łi'didlii nakhwaagwaldak geenjit juk nakhwajyaa ihłii kwaa? 
48:4:017:0	Zhit juu geech'ǫąąhtan adaa'įį nąįį gwintł'oo diitee ohjyaa nakhwagoonyąą t'iginchy'aa, gaa deenakhwagahnyąą ąįį łyaa gwiizųų, shatr'ahoh'ee geenjit shrahtee nakhwageheelyaa t'igii'in. 
48:4:018:0	Gaa gwinzii eenjit gwintł'oo diitee ohjyaa nakhwagoonyąą ji' t'ee gweheezyaa, nakhwaa dhiidii dąį' gwizhrįh nakwaa gaa jii t'ee khit ts'ą' łi'didlii gwanlii t'oonchy'aa. 
48:4:019:0	Shigii gwilee nąįį! Christ vik'it t'ahohchy'aa gwats'ą' tr'injaa tr'iinin haa iłts'ik agwąhtł'oo nakhweenjit shaashraa neehaatth'ak. 
48:4:002:1	Tr'injaa Hagar ts'ą' Sarah haa T'igiizhik Gwik'it
48:4:020:0	Juk nakhwaa dhiidii ji' nihthan, gwinzii nakhwats'ą' gihihkhyaa geenjit. Jaghaii jyaa dakhwazhik gaashandaii kwaa! 
48:4:021:0	Nakhwatee juu Moses va-law k'it oihshyaa niihthan nąįį nakhwaahałkat, it'ee lee ąįį Law deenyąą t'inyąą gaakhwandaii? 
48:4:022-023:0	Ąįį Law zhit jyaa digwinyąą: Abraham vidinji' neekwaii gwiinli'. Tr'injaa Hagar slave nilii ts'an vidinji' ch'ihłak vagoodlit, gaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa, Abraham, Hagar haa nihłohjii goovaiinyą' kwaa. Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Abraham veenjit deehee'yaa yaiinyą' ąįį eenjit chan ch'izhii vidinji' chan va'at Sarah slave nilii kwaa ts'an vagoodlit. 
48:4:024:0	Jii t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa deehee'yaa nyąą ąįį gwik'it tr'injaa neekwaii nąįį gwandak t'arahnyąą. Ch'ihłak Hagar oozhii, Mt. Sinai gwats'an nilii, vigii nąįį slaves giinlii. 
48:4:025:0	Hagar t'ee jii kwaii gwik'it nilii, ddhah Sinai t'ee Arabia nahkat gwizhit gwats'an law tr'iłtsąįį ts'ą' juk drin kwaiik'it gwachoo goo'aii Jerusalem, ąįį gwich'in nąįį Moses va-law ts'ą' slave giinlii.
