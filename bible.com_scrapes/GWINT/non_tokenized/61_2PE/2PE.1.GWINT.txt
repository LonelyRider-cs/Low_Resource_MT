61:1:001:0	Shįį t'ee Simon Peter nakhwats'ą' ch'adįhtł'oo, Jesus Christ vitsyaa ts'ą' veenjit Tr'ihił'e' ihłii.Zhit juu Christ k'injiighit nąįį nakhwats'ą' ch'adįhtł'oo. Jesus, Diti' vik'eegwahthat k'iighai', Dii-Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ts'ą' Jesus Christ haa neediinahshii. Jii vik'injigwiighit hil'ee diinantł'eiin'ąįį, ąįį nakhwan chan gwik'it t'ohchy'aa. 
61:1:001:1	Gwik'injigwiighit nąįį, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa K'it T'igihee'yaa Eenjit.
61:1:002:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa, Jesus Diik'eegwaadhat haa goovaanandaii k'iighai' ch'eegoonzhrįį, tsinehdan haa nakhwats'an oolį'. 
61:1:003:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vandee ts'ą' gwinzii gwizhrįh t'igwii'in dąį' diinoozhrii ąįįt'ee Jesus vat'aii haa t'igwii'in gaagwiindaii. Vich'eegwąhndit ts'ą' gwinzii t'ii'in kwaii datthak diits'an iłtsąįį, vik'it t'igwehee'yaa eenjit. 
61:1:004:0	Jii jyąhts'ą' t'ee gwihil'ee gwachoh diinantł'eegwehee'aa nyąą gwik'it t'iizhik, jii kwaii eh'an nankat gwiizųų t'agǫǫ'ya' gwiindhan kwaii datthak akhagooraheenjyaa ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vik'it t'igweheechy'aa goo'aii. 
61:1:005:0	Jii kwaii geh'an gwinzii t'akhwa'in gwik'eegǫhdaii ts'ą' nakhwak'injigwiighit gwandaa nitsii ohtsii, ąįį nitsii dhohtsąįį tł'ęę t'ee gwandaa gaandaii choh hohłyaa. 
61:1:006:0	Gaandaii choh dhoodlit tł'ęę deekhwa'in datthak adak'ǫǫntii; adak'ǫǫntii łyaa akhagohnyą' shrǫ', ts'ą' akhagahohjyaa kwaa ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vik'it t'igweheechy'aa gwizhrįh vaa ǫhłįį. 
61:1:007:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa zhrįh vaa ǫhłįį ts'ą' gwik'injiighit nąįį ch'eet'igwiniindhan gooveenjit khwa'įį, nakhwalak nąįį eet'ohthan gwik'it gooveet'ohthan. 
61:1:008:0	Jii kwaii gaakhwandaii łyaa gwat'oohchy'aa, khit ts'ą' vaakhwa'įį ji', Diik'eegwaadhat Jesus Christ vaakhwandaii gwizhit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa veenjit gwiizųų tr'agwahoh'yaa kwaa gwits'į' nakhwats'iheenjyaa eenjit t'ihnyąą. 
61:1:009:0	Ąįį kwaii ch'ihłee yii'įį kwaa ji', nahgwan gwizhrįh nakhwaagweech'in ts'ą' nakhwandee naatthaa k'it t'igweheechy'aa, yeenii nakhwatr'agwaanduu nakhweenjit ginildee kwaa k'it t'akhwa'in. 
61:1:010:0	Shalak gwik'injiighit nąįį, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa gwitee nakhoozhrii ts'ą' tr'anakhwahchįį deekhwa'ya' nakhoonyaa izhit khit gwik'it gǫhdaii, jyaa dohchy'aa ji' nakhwak'injigwiighit duuyeh akhagoohnyąą. 
61:1:011:0	Jyąhts'ą' t'ee Diik'eegwaadhat, Jesus Christ neediinahahshii, ts'ą' Veegwinii'ee ndaagwąą'ąį' kwaa łyaa gwintł'oo veechįį nihdeehohdaa t'oonchy'aa. 
61:1:012:0	Ch'adąį' hee vaakhwandaii ts'ą' gwik'it t'ohchy'aa gaa jii deenakhwaiinyą' geenjit khit gineenakhwahałdaii geenjit. 
61:1:013:0	Deegwąhkhyuk gwįįhdaii datthak, jii kwaii geenjit gineehoondaii gwizhrįh gweheezyaa. 
61:1:014:0	Jii shatthąį' it'ee niighit kwaa hahchyaa eenjit Diik'eegwaadhat Jesus Christ gwinzii shaagwildak. 
61:1:002:1	Lęįį Nąįį Christ Vich'eegwąhndit Dagandee Haa Googwąąh'ya'
61:1:015:0	Shat'aii datthak haa jii kwaii gineehoondee kwaa ąįį eenjit gwik'eegwihįhdaii, shihehkwaa gwats'ą' datthak. 
61:1:016:0	T'aih haa Diik'eegwaadhat Jesus Christ k'ineeheedik jyaa nakhwaihnyąą dąį' ąįį zhyaa k'ii'an gwandak tr'ahtsii t'igwinyąą kwaa, diinandee haa gwihil'ee gwarąąh'ya' t'igwinyąą. 
61:1:017-018:0	Ch'eegwąhndit akwat hil'ee haa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Gwiti' yintł'eegwiin'ąįį dąį' ddhah shroodiinyąą kat tr'iilk'įį gwiizhit t'igwiizhik. Izhit dąį' ch'eegwąhndit gwachoh k'iidąą goo'aii zhit gwats'an jyaa digwinyąą, “Jii t'ee Shidinji', veet'iihthan łyaa vashoo'ahłįį!” Zheekat gwats'an jyaa digwinyąą gwiriitth'ak. 
61:1:019:0	Yeenaa dąį' Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii Nąįį deegiinyą' Jesus eeginkhe' łyaa łi'didlii gaagwiindaii. Jii ginjik gwinzii gwadǫǫhk'įį ji' gweheezyaa, tǫǫ neech'arahdrii k'it t'inchy'aa, gwizhrįh hee gwigweheechy'aa, gineech'ihee'aa ts'ą' są' nakhwadrii zhit neech'ahaahdrii t'ee Jesus t'inchy'aa. 
61:1:020:0	Ch'ihłee gaa duuyeh ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nąįį deeginyą' t'igwinyąą gaagwiindaii kwaa izhit ganoondaii, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa goots'ą' gwizhrįh nagwa'ak. 
61:1:021:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nąįį deegiinyą' t'ee Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit gwats'an gwizhrįh t'igwinyąą, dinjii ts'ąnh t'igwinyąą kwaa gaa Ch'anky'aa Shroodiinyąą zhit gwats'an gwizhrįh, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa viginjik eegoogwaandak t'iginyąą.
