58:2:001:1	Neereheezhii ąįįt'ee Jiintsii
58:2:001:0	Jyąhts'ą' geh'an jidii tr'iitth'ak ąįį łi'deegwidlii, ąįį łahchy'aa gwint'aii tr'ooheentą' gwizhrįh goo'aii t'oonchy'aa. Diigwik'injigwiighit akharooheenjyaa kwaa geenjit. 
58:2:002:0	Zheekat gwich'in Moses va-law yeenii diilak nąįį antł'eegwiin'ąįį t'ee łi'deegwidlii gwigweech'in, ts'ą' dinjii nąįį deegihee'yaa goo'aii gwik'it t'igii'in kwaa ts'ą' yik'eegwahthat kwaa nąįį t'ee nats'ąą khaiinjich'igiheeghit gwik'it khaiinjich'agooriłthat t'igiinchy'aa. 
58:2:003:0	Nats'ąą gwik'iighai' khaiinjich'iriighit gwats'an tr'ineereheedaa ąįį neereheezhii gwadrąįį gwatsal gaa ninjich'aradhat kwaa ji'? Ąįį K'eegwaadhat Jesus adan tr'ookit it'ee neereheezhii jiintsii geegiinkhe', ąįįts'ą' juu zhat dąį' yiitth'ak nąįį datthak diits'ą' łi'deegwidlii gwigwigweełkįį. 
58:2:002:1	Juu Neereheezhii gwats'ą' Diinehee'aa
58:2:004:0	Ąįį jyaa digwii'in gwiizhik Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa chan gwigwii'in ts'ą' nindal gwaahchy'aa nihłehjuk t'iichy'aa kwaii datthak haa gwigweech'in gwahtsii, ąįįts'ą' Ch'anky'aa Shroodiinyąą k'iighai' vit'eegwahahchy'aa eenjit nats'ąą nihłehjuk gwitr'it t'agwagwahah'yaa goovantł'eiinlii. 
58:2:005:0	Ąįį nankat k'eejit neegweheelyaa gwinyąą ts'ą' geegarinkhii, ąįįt'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa dizheekat gwich'in ąįį k'eegwagwahahthat geenjit zhat nagoovinlii kwaa. 
58:2:006:0	Gaa, Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit gwa'an jyaa digwinyąą,“Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa jiintsii jaghaii jii nankat gwidinjii nąįį gooninjich'ahthat?Jaghaii gook'ąąhtii? 
58:2:007:0	Ch'ihłee gwatsal zheekat gwich'in nąįį andaa gwintsal gihil'ee kwaa goovołtsąįį,ts'ą' ch'eegwąhndit ts'ą' gwihil'ee haa ch'ihłee goots'an dhąhtsąįį, 
58:2:008:0	ts'ą' jidii datthak ts'ą' k'eedeegwahaadhat gooviłtsąįį.”Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa datthak gwats'ą' k'eedeegwagwahaadhat gooviłtsąįį gwinyąą, ąįįt'ee jidii datthak t'arahnyąą. Gaa, juk gweendaa dzaa nankat gwidinjii jidii datthak gwats'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat gwaraah'in kwaa. 
58:2:009:0	Gaa Jesus, ąįį zheekat gwich'in nąįį andaa gwintsal hil'ee tr'iłtsąįį, ąįį gwigweech'in gwaraah'in, jyąhts'ą' gwik'iighai' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vich'eegoonzhrii zhit juu nąįį datthak eenjit niindhat, ts'ą' khaiinjich'ahohthat ts'ą' niindhat geh'an juk gwihil'ee ts'ą' ch'eegwąhndit vantł'eeriin'ąįį, gwaraah'in. 
58:2:010:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa juu jidii datthak gwiłtsąįį ts'ą' nihłoontą' iłtsąįį geh'an, Jesus khaiinjich'aheeghit k'iighai', gweheezyaa gwiłtsąįį, digii lęįį nąįį ts'an dich'eegwąhndit lat goots'an iltsąįį ts'ą' Jesus t'ee neereheezhii gwats'ą' diineelyaa t'inchy'aa. 
58:2:011:0	Ąįį haa it'ee Jesus dzaa nankat gwidinjii nąįį tr'agwaanduu gwats'an shroodiinyąą gooviłtsąįį, ts'ą' adan ts'ą' geedan haa datthak shroodiinyąą ginlii ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa gooti' nilii. Izhik geh'an Jesus shizhehk'aa gwich'in ǫhłįį gavahnyąą ts'ą' goo'oozhii ahłįį kwaa t'inchy'aa. 
58:2:012:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa jyaa dahnyąą,“Shizhehk'aa nąįį deenizhit datthak gavaa gwahaldak t'oonchy'aa nyąąts'ą' nijin khaihłan nineegiidal dąį' gootee gwats'an nahał'aa t'oonchy'aa!” yahnyąą. 
58:2:013:0	Jii chan jyaa nyąą,“Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ąįį vit'injihaałchy'aa.”Ąįįts'ą' jii chan jyaa nyąą,“Ąįį tr'iinin Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa shantł'inlii nąįį haa datthak dzaa gavaa t'ihchy'aa.” 
58:2:014:0	Jii tr'iinin shigii gooveegirinkhii nąįį dzaa nankat gwats'an geedaa ts'ą' goodaa ts'ą' gootthąį' haa gwanlii, Jesus, adan t'ee gook'it t'inchy'aa ts'ą' dzaa nankat gwidinjii dhidlit. Niindhat gwik'iighai' Ch'anky'aa Tr'aanduu an hahtsyaa, Tr'aanduu ąįįt'ee ch'ichį' ts'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat. 
58:2:015:0	ts'ą' jyąhts'ą' gwik'iighai' juu tr'agwaanduu goots'ą' k'eedeegwaadhat nąįį gwats'an tr'iinlii, ch'ichį' agąąnjat geh'an. 
58:2:016:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee zheekat gwich'in nąįį Jesus goots'iheenjyaa geenjit dzaa gwats'ą' haazhii t'ii'in kwaa gwizhrįh gwigweech'in, gaa Abraham ts'an deedhaa nąįį ts'iheenjyaa ąįį geenjit dzaa gwats'ą' haazhii t'ii'in. 
58:2:017:0	Dzaa nankat dinjii nąįį k'it yiłtsąįį ts'ą' nats'ahts'ą' datthak diik'it yiłtsąįį, gwinyąą, jyąhts'ą' gwik'iighai' gooveenjit łi'deegwidlii vit'injiraahchy'aa heelyaa ts'ą' neeshraahchy'aa chan heeghyaa gwizhit ndak khii giinkhih choh, ąįį nilii gwizhik Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa eenjit tr'agwahaah'yaa, jyąhts'ą' gwik'iighai' chan dinjii nąįį atr'agwaanduu oo'an gwahaadlii geenjit t'iizhik. 
58:2:018:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee zhit juu juk tr'agwaanduu t'asho'ya' niindhan nąįį ts'iheenjyaa jyaa digihee'yaa kwaa geenjit adan t'ee tsinteetr'eelkaii ts'ą' khaiinjich'akhwadhat, dzaa nankat k'eekhwazhii dąį'
