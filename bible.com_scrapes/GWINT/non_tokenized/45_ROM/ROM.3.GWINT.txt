45:3:001:0	Jews nąįį, Jews nilii kwaa nąįį andaa ginzįį lee t'oonchy'aa? Jews nąįį goodhah gihłeerahaht'ii ąįį gwihil'ee lee t'igii'in? 
45:3:002:0	Aahą', łyaa nats'ąą goo'aii datthak gwik'it gwinzii goo'ee t'oonchy'aa. Tr'ookit dąį' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Jews nąįį ts'ą' khaginkhee ąįį gwandaa gwihil'ee t'oonchy'aa. 
45:3:003:0	Gaa goolat nąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa akhagoonyąą ji' yu'? Ąįį ji' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa chan akhagavohoonjyaa lee t'oonchy'aa? 
45:3:004:0	Łyaa zhyaa nakwaa! Dzaa nankat dinjii nąįį datthak oots'it ji' gaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ąįį łyaa łi'didlii nilii t'oonchy'aa. Dęhtły'aa Choh kat gwadanakhwatł'oo,“Nijin Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ginkhii dąį' łi'deegwidlii haa ginkhii gwigweech'in. Juu gwiizųų nizhit nahnyąą chan gaiiyiigoovahohtsyaa gwizhrįh.” 
45:3:005:0	Gaa gwiizųų t'igwii'in ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa gwinzii t'ii'in andaa gwigweech'in ji' yu'? Geenjit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa diigwąąhshii dąį' lee gwiizųų t'ii'in gweheenjyaa? (Dinjii, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haandaii kwaa k'it giihkhii t'ihnyąą.) 
45:3:006:0	Łyaa zhyaa nakwaa! Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa łi'didlii kwaa ji' nats'ahts'ą' jii nan ts'an dinjii nizįį akwat dinjii iizųų nąįį tr'iheelyaa? 
45:3:007:0	Dinjii ch'ihłee duulee jii jyaa diheenjyaa, “Gaa gwintł'oo oihts'it ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vich'eegwąhndit gwandaa gwizhrįh gwagwahch'in ji' yu'? Jaghaii shatr'agwaanduu eenjit khaiinjich'isheheeghit?” 
45:3:001:1	Ch'ihłee Gaa Khit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa K'eegwahthat Kwaa
45:3:008:0	Jii jyaa dagoonyą' jaghaii, “Gweheezyaa geenjit tr'agwaanduu t'agǫǫ'ya' jaghaii?” Dinjii lat nąįį sheegiginkhii ts'ą' jyaa dihnyąą shagahnyąą, ąįį nąįį t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nats'ahts'ą' gooveegoo'aii gwik'it googwahąhshii t'iginchy'aa. 
45:3:009:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee Jews nąįį lee Jews nilii kwaa nąįį andaa ginzįį? Łyaa zhyaa nakwaa! Jews ts'ą' Jews nilii kwaa haa nihłaa gootr'agwaanduu gwanlii t'oonchy'aa. 
45:3:010:0	Dęhtły'aa Choh kat gwadanakhwatł'oo,“Ch'ihłee gaa vatr'agwaanduu gwakwaa t'oonchy'aa. 
45:3:011:0	Ch'ihłee gaa vigwizhi' gwanliits'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vatąįį hoihshi' niindhan kwaa. 
45:3:012:0	Datthak ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vits'į' tr'aa'ee,ts'ą' tr'agwaanduu t'igwii'in.Ch'ihłee gaa gwinzii t'ii'in kwaa.Ch'ihłak gaa nakwaa. 
45:3:013:0	Digiginjik haa tr'agwaanduu giginkhii,digiighit haa khan goots'it geenjit giizųų.Goodeevyaa kat gyųų iizųų poison dha'aii k'it t'inchy'aa. 
45:3:014:0	Digiginjik haa dinjii nąįį ts'ą' tr'agwaanduu gwizhrįh t'igihee'yaa ginyąą. 
45:3:015:0	Nagoogwal'in kwaa haa dinjii nąįį khaiinjich'igahkhit ts'ą' giiłkhwąįį. 
45:3:016:0	Nijin geedaa datthak dinjii khaiinjich'igahkhit ts'ą' an googahtsii. 
45:3:017:0	Dinjii tsinehdan zhit nihłaa gwich'įį kwaa gwagwahtsii. 
45:3:018:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa łyaa vagwaanjat ąįį zhit gaagiindaii kwaa.” 
45:3:019:0	Jews nąįį Moses va-law zhit deegwehee'yaa gwinyąą gwik'it dinjii giheelyaa gaagwiindaii. Ąįįts'ą' digidrii zhit Law haagiindaii geh'an Ch'aroahkat Drin zhit ji' nankat datthak dinjii ch'ihłak gaa duuyeh Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa doo'ya' goovoonyąą gaashandaii kwaa ginyąą. 
45:3:002:1	Nats'ahts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Vaa Gwinzii Tr'eedaa
45:3:020:0	Law kat deegwehee'yaa gwinyąą ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vik'eegwarahthat t'igwinyąą kwaa, gaa tr'agwaanduu diigwitr'it gwanlii ąįį gwigweheechy'aa geenjit t'oonchy'aa. 
45:3:021:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vaagwinzii tr'iheedaa juk diits'ą' gwizhrįh nagwaatth'at ts'ą' Law zhit deegwehee'yaa gwinyąą t'agwarahnyąą kwaa, gaa Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit Moses va-law ts'ą' yeenaa dąį' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Eenjit Ginkhii nąįį haa geegwagwaandak gwik'it. 
45:3:022:0	Dinjii nats'ahts'ą' Jesus Christ k'injiighit k'iighai' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa goovaa gwinzii gwahtsii. Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa juu Christ k'injiighit nąįį jyaa dąh'in, datthak veenjit nihłehjuk t'iginchy'aa kwaa geh'an. 
45:3:023:0	Juu nąįį datthak gootr'agwaanduu gwanlii, ts'ą' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vich'eegoonzhrii eenjit giinzii kwaa. 
45:3:024:0	Gaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ąįį Christ Jesus k'injiriighit k'iighai' diitr'agwaanduu gwats'an tr'ineediiniinlii, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa dich'eegoonzhrįį haa jidii zhyaa, diinantł'eegwiin'ąįį ąįį haa vaa gwinzii tr'iheedaa geenjit gwats'an tr'ineediiniinlii t'oonchy'aa. 
45:3:025:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa diits'ą' zhyaants'įį ąįį akhagooheenjyaa geenjit Jesus, diitr'agwaanduu eenjit niheedhaa diits'ą' naachįį. Nijin Jesus diineenjit niindhat gwik'injiriighit dąį' t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vaa gwinzii tr'eedaa. Jii jyaa diizhit t'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa khit gwinzii t'ii'in gwagwahch'in geenjit, niighit dąį' nijin dinjii nąįį haa khalchį' kwaa nilii ts'ą' tr'agwaanduu t'igii'in geenjit googwahąhshii dąį'. 
45:3:026:0	Ts'ą' juk Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa gwinzii khit t'ii'in gwagwaahkįį, nijin juu tr'agwaanduu t'ii'in nąįį Jesus k'injigiighit k'iighai' yiheedaa gwiłtsąįį dąį'. 
45:3:027:0	Akwat jidii eenjit ideech'irehee'yaa? Jaghaii ideech'irehee'yaa? Law kat nats'ąą dinjii tr'iheelyaa gwinyąą gwik'it dinjii tr'iinlii geh'an lee? Nakwaa, gaa gwik'injiriighit geh'an gwizhrįh ideech'irehee'yaa. 
45:3:028:0	Ąįį geh'an dinjii vigwik'injigwiighit haa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haa gwinzii ahaa. Law k'eegwahthat ąįį geh'an nakwaa. 
45:3:029:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa lee Jews nąįį zhrįh eenjit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nilii? Nakwaa! Jews nilii kwaa nąįį eenjit chan Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nilii t'inchy'aa. 
45:3:030:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa ch'ihłak zhrįh gwanlii t'oonchy'aa, Jews chan ts'ą' Jews nilii kwaa nąįį haa googwik'injigwiighit k'iighai' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa haa gwinzii giheedaa goovahtsii. 
45:3:031:0	Gwik'injiriighit geh'an lee Law akharooheenjyaa? Łyaa nakwaa, gaa gwik'injiriighit k'iighai', gwandaa gwinzii Law k'eegwarahahthat t'oonchy'aa.
