47:1:009:0	Łyaa diidrin ndaanaan'ąį' gweheelyaa gwiindhan. Gaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa juu niindhat nąįį ineegwiłdaii, adan zhrįh vit'injirahahchy'aa geenjit jyąhts'ą' goodlit.
