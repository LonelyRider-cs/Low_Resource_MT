60:4:001:1	Gwik'injohkhit Haa Ch'ijuk Gǫhdaii
60:4:001:0	Christ datthąį' zhit khaiinjich'iindhat gwik'it nakhwan t'ee nakhwatthąį' zhit khaiinjich'anakhwarahkhit ji' t'ee nakhwatr'agwaanduu gweheekwaa. Nats'ąą khaiinjich'iindhat gininjich'ohthat gwik'iighai' nakhwat'aii neegohtsii. 
60:4:002:0	Juk gwats'an łyaa zhyaa Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nats'ahts'ą' jii nankat gǫhdaii nakhoonyaa gwik'it goohǫhdaii gwizhrįh, nakhwan nats'ahts'ą' gǫhdaii nohthan gwik'it nakwaa. 
60:4:003:0	Yeenii juu gwik'injiighit kwaa ts'ą' googwitr'it tr'agwaanduu nąįį k'it t'akhwa'ya', gwiizųų gwizhrįh t'agǫǫ'ya' nohthan, dineech'oonjik ts'ą' chan khaihłan nineekhwadal ts'ą' nihts'ą' gwiizųų t'akhwa'in, ohłęįį ts'ą' ch'akhwanįį geenjit khaihłan nineekhwadal, ch'ik'itiltsį' zhrį' ts'ą' chan khagadǫhjii jii kwaii duuyeh gwik'it t'akhwa'in goo'aii. 
60:4:004:0	Ąįįts'ą' t'ee zhit juu gwik'injiighit kwaa nąįį gook'it t'akhwa'in kwaa dąį' łyaa gwik'injigiighit kwaa ts'ą' gwiizųų nakhwats'ą' giginkhii. 
60:4:005:0	Gaa niighit kwaa ąįį Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Ch'aroahkat Drin ji' goovahaahkat ts'ą' juu gwandaii akwat niindhat haa nihteetr'iheelyaa. 
60:4:002:1	Jidii Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Diinantł'eegwin'ąįį Ąįį Gwinzii T'igwii'in Geenjit Gwizhrįh Vit'eegwahahchy'aa T'inchy'aa
60:4:006:0	Izhit geh'an juu niindhat nąįį gaa goots'ą' ginkhe' ąįį Gwandak Nizįį goovaagwaandak geenjit. Juu jii nankat gwandaii nąįį nihteetr'ilii ts'ą' daganky'aa zhit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa k'it gogweheendaii geenjit. Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa, Dinjii nąįį datthak nihteetr'iheelyaa gaagwiindaii. 
60:4:007:0	Nan ndaanąą'ąį' gwats'ą' niighit kwaa t'oonchy'aa, khagadǫhjii ts'ą' deekhwa'in gwak'ǫǫhtii gwiizhit, nǫhkhii shrǫ'. 
60:4:008:0	Jii t'ee łyaa gwintsii geegoo'aii t'igwehee'yaa goo'aii! Nihłeenjit ch'eet'igwiniindhan akhwa'įį. Ch'eet'igwiniindhan zhrįh nihłeenjit khwa'įį dąį', gweedhaa datthak nakhwats'ą' tr'agwaanduu t'ii'in nąįį gooveenjit gineehoondee. 
60:4:009:0	K'eegwiichy'aa t'ohnyąą kwaa ts'ą' juu gwik'injiighit nąįį nakhwazheh nihdǫhchik. 
60:4:010:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nihłehjuk gwinzii t'akhwa'in nakhwantł'eegwiin'ąįį, ąįį vaa nihłits'igweheenjyaa t'inchy'aa. 
60:4:003:1	Gwik'injigwiighit Geenjit Khaiinjich'iriighit
60:4:011:0	Giinkhih nąįį, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Viginjik eech'oohtan; juu vitsyaa nilii nąįį, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa t'aih nakhwantł'eiin'ąįį haa vitsyaa ǫhłįį. Jidii datthak zhit Jesus Christ vik'iighai' Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa deerahąąh'aa. Adan zhrįh khit ts'ą' khit geenjit vich'eegwahdrii ts'ą' t'aih haa vats'an nilii! Amen!! 
60:4:012:0	Shijyaa gwilee nąįį, gwinlęįį geegwaroolii kwaa nakhwadrii zhit gwiizųų nakhwats'ą' t'igwehee'yaa, juk gwizhrįh nakhwats'ą' jyaa digwehee'yaa kwaa t'oonchy'aa gaa nakhwak'injigwiighit łi'didlii ji' geenjit t'igwii'in. 
60:4:013:0	Christ khaiinjich'akhwadhat gwik'it nakhwan chan khaiinjich'akhwaghit ąįį geenjit shoo ǫhłįį, nijin vich'eegwąhndit gwizhrįh nagwaatth'at ji' łyaa nakhwaashrigweheechy'aa. 
60:4:014:0	Christ vik'injuhkhit geenjit gwiizųų nakhwats'ą' girinkhii ji' geenjit shoo ǫhłįį, ąįįt'ee Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa vich'eegwąhndit nakhwaa nilii t'oonchy'aa. 
60:4:015:0	Khaiinjich'akhwaghit ji', dinjii ęhdaa tr'agǫh'ąįį, ch'oh'įį akwat nakhweh'an gwiizųų t'igwii'in akwat nakhwadraii t'igwii'in kwaa gwa'an gaanagoh'ąįį, jii kwaii eenjit khaiinjich'akhwaghit shrǫ'! 
60:4:016:0	Gaa gwik'injuhkhit geenjit khaiinjich'anakhwarahthat ji' gwa'oozhii ǫhłį' shrǫ' gaa Christ vats'an ǫhłįį geenjit Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa mahsį' vohnyąą. 
60:4:017:0	It'ee khaiinjii Ch'aroahkat Drin nigwiindhat ts'ą' gwik'injiighit nąįį tr'ookit tr'oohaahkat. Diikhwan tr'ookit diiroohahkat ji', juu Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa Vagwaandak Nizįį k'injiighit kwaa nąįį nats'ąą teeheedhat li'? 
60:4:018:0	Dęhtły'aa Choh zhit jyaa t'igwinyąą,“Dinjii nizįį nąįį gaa neeheezhii geenjit gogwantrii,akwat zhit juu Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nehts'į' nilii nąįį ts'ą' tr'agwaanduu tr'agwah'in nąįį goodivee deegweheechy'aa li'?” 
60:4:019:0	Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa deekhwa'ya' nakhoonyaa izhit geh'an khaiinjich'anakhwahthat ji' nihk'it gwinzii tr'agoh'in, Vit'eegwijyąhchy'aa nakhwiłtsąįį vintł'eedoochįį, nakhweenjit deeshi'yaa nakhwaiinyą' chy'aa khit gwik'it t'ihee'yaa t'inyąą.
