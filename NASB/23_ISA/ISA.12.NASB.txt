23:12:001:1	Thanksgiving Expressed
23:12:001:0	Then you will say on that day , “ I will give thanks to You , O Lord ; For although You were angry with me , Your anger is turned away , And You comfort me .
23:12:002:0	Behold , God is my salvation , I will trust and not be afraid ; For the Lord God is my strength and song , And He has become my salvation . ”
23:12:003:0	Therefore you will joyously draw waterFrom the springs of salvation .
23:12:004:0	And in that day you will say , “ Give thanks to the Lord , call on His name . Make known His deeds among the peoples ; Make them remember that His name is exalted . ”
23:12:005:0	Praise the Lord in song , for He has done excellent things ; Let this be known throughout the earth .
23:12:006:0	Cry aloud and shout for joy , O inhabitant of Zion , For great in your midst is the Holy One of Israel .
