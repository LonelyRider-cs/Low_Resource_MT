19:100:001:1	PSALM 100
19:100:002:1	All Men Exhorted to Praise God .
19:100:003:1	A Psalm for Thanksgiving .
19:100:001:0	Shout joyfully to the Lord , all the earth .
19:100:002:0	Serve the Lord with gladness ; Come before Him with joyful singing .
19:100:003:0	Know that the Lord Himself is God ; It is He who has made us , and not we ourselves ; We are His people and the sheep of His pasture .
19:100:004:0	Enter His gates with thanksgiving And His courts with praise . Give thanks to Him , bless His name .
19:100:005:0	For the Lord is good ; His lovingkindness is everlastingAnd His faithfulness to all generations .
